How to Test the Application

Step 1: Open the Application

The dev server is running at: http://localhost:5173/

Open this URL in your browser.

Step 2: Open Browser Console

Press F12 (or right-click ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí Console tab)

Step 3: Trigger the Drawer

Copy and paste this command in the console:

window.dispatchEvent(new Event("spine-age:open"))

The drawer should slide up from the bottom (mobile) or from the side (desktop).

Step 4: Complete the Assessment

Step 1 - Daily Observations: Answer 5 Yes/No questions

Step 2 - Posture Assessment: 6 questions with 3 options each

Step 3 - Range of Motion: 5 questions with 3 options each

Step 4 - Functional Tests: 3 questions with 3 options each

Step 5 - Tenderness: 3 questions with 3 options each

Step 6 - Height Loss: 1 question with 2 options

Step 7 - Your Details: Fill in name, email, and phone

Step 5: View Results

After submitting Step 7, you should see:

  • ‚úÖ Checkmark animation
  • Your name displayed
  • Total score out of 41
  • Score interpretation (Excellent/Good/Moderate/High)
  • Visual score bar

Step 6: Test localStorage

1. Start answering questions but don't complete

2. Close the drawer

3. Reopen the drawer (run the console command again)

4. Your previous answers should be restored!

Step 7: Close the Drawer

To close the drawer, run this in console:

window.dispatchEvent(new Event("spine-age:close"))

Or click outside the drawer area.
